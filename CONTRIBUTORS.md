## How to contribute

Contributions are accepted under the terms of the project's BSD 3-Clause License. By submitting a pull request, contributors agree that their work may be included under the same license. Contributors retain copyright in their contributions unless explicitly transferred.

When submitting a contribution:
1. Add yourself to this fil under the appropriate year, with a brief description of your changes.
2. Update the copyright header in each modified file, adding a new line on top to include the current year:
   ```
   Copyright (c) YYYY Dolby Laboratories and contributors
   ```
   where YYYY is the current year.
